I have been a qualified Complementary Therapist for many years. During that time I have successfully worked with a wide range of people and conditions and feel privileged to have been able to help so many people within my community.

Working on my own children made me realise that my interest lay in working with families. I have developed my skills and qualifications to specialise in the area of Fertility, Maternity, Babies and children. I am passionate about my work and my clients.

I live in the South West in Devon and my clinic is based at the Exeter Natural Health Centre in Queens Walk - between Queen Street and Gandy Street - a tranquil haven right in the heart of the city. The Centre offers a relaxing area, with refreshments, where you can sit quietly after your treatment before returning to the hustle and bustle of life.
